## Description

Unlock endless painting inspiration for your miniatures with Minispire – your AI-powered creative companion for tabletop hobby enthusiasts.

**GENERATE INSPIRATION**
Stuck on a color scheme? Describe your vision and let AI generate stunning reference images tailored to your miniatures. Upload a photo of your model and explore new color possibilities.

**AI PAINTING COACH**
Get expert advice from your personal painting advisor. Ask about techniques, color theory, paint brands (Citadel, Vallejo, Army Painter, Scale75), and more. Whether you're a beginner or veteran painter, get the guidance you need.

**SAVE YOUR IDEAS**
Build your personal inspiration gallery. Save generated images, tag them by project, and favorite your best finds for quick reference at your painting station.

**FEATURES**
• AI-powered image generation for painting references
• Personal painting coach with expert knowledge
• Support for popular paint brands and color matching
• Local gallery to save and organize inspirations
• Dark theme optimized for any lighting
• Works offline for saved content

Perfect for Warhammer 40K, Age of Sigmar, D&D, Pathfinder, historical wargaming, and any miniature painting project.
