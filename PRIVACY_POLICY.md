# Privacy Policy for Minispire

**Last Updated: January 23, 2026**

## Introduction

Minispire ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Camera and Photo Library Access
- **Purpose**: We request access to your camera and photo library to let you take photos of your miniatures or select reference images for AI-powered painting inspiration.
- **Storage**: Photos you select are processed temporarily to generate inspiration. We do not store your photos on our servers.

### AI-Generated Content
- **Image Generation**: When you use our AI image generation feature, your text prompts are sent to Google's Gemini AI service to generate images.
- **Chat Conversations**: When you use our AI painting coach, your messages are sent to Google's Gemini AI service to provide personalized advice.

### Local Data Storage
- **Saved Inspirations**: Images you choose to save are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage.
- **Preferences**: App settings and preferences are stored locally on your device.

## Third-Party Services

### Google Firebase
We use Google Firebase for:
- App initialization and configuration
- AI services (Gemini) for image generation and chat functionality

Google's privacy policy applies to data processed by their services: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Sharing

We do not:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
- Store your photos or conversations on our servers beyond temporary processing

## Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Saved inspirations remain on your device until you delete them
- **AI Processing**: Prompts and images are processed in real-time and not retained by us after generation

## Your Rights

You can:
- Delete saved inspirations at any time within the app
- Revoke camera/photo library permissions through your device settings
- Request information about your data by contacting us

## Children's Privacy

Minispire is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.
